OUR TRIP CATEGORIES

Find out what kind of adventure is perfect for you

STUNNING SCENERY

Water, fire and ice have combined to make some of the most spectacular scenery in the world -- from giant crystal caves to mud volcanoes and rock formations that look like works of art. If your office and daily commute aren't a fitting reminder of the extraordinary natural diversity of planet earth, go out for some inspiration from these incredible places.

RELAX TOURS

If you are looking to disconnect from everyday life or to recharge your batteries, maybe an action-packed itinerary can be too much. Never fear, we have the right choice for you, from tropical islands to laid-back cityscapes, you deserve to go somewhere that matches your definition of relaxing and unwinding.

ACTIVE TRIPS

Active adventures get you closer to the destination by letting you hike, bike, and multisport your way through it. We provide the equipment, the experts, and the opportunity, you provide the energy.

Biking, hiking, whitewater rafting, and other so-amazing-they-need-a-hyphen activities await. Come and get ‘em.

EPIC JOURNEYS

There is something intrinsically satisfying about making an epic journey. It is travel in its purest form, movement for movement’s sake. The sights along the way are important, they give the whole thing some context, but the real reward is just making it from A to B. Or A to Z, rather.

Forging a trail across some of the most captivating landscapes on earth, these are adventures that will live in the memory long after the dust of the trail has settled.
